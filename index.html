About Me

sivakarthikeyan is an indian actor,singer,film producer,lyricist,and television presenter, who works predominantly in tamil films.he produces films under his production company(Sivakarthikeyan productions) sivakarthikeyan has a nick name of SK.He started his acting career in 2012(Marina).On the topic of his appearance, Sivakarthikeyan has that unique mix of humble, impish and naughty looks which work in great favor for him to play a variety of characters. In Tamil films, it is advantageous to have a Dravidian look that would go well, especially while essaying rural and semi urban roles. Later recognising his talents, he started doing other shows such as “Siricha pochu” and “Adhu idhu edhu” . This brought him a lot of fame. Later he started to be featured in a lot of shows in Vijay TV, made a lot of people laugh and became one of the important icons of Vijay TV.
